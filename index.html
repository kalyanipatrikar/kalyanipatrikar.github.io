About me

I explore nanoscale phenomenon in electronic devices, to gain insights into charge transport mechanisms, and find ways to enhance their performance.

My approach combines fabrication and characterization of devices to extract information about their working, with first ptinciples simulations to reveal phenomenon at molecular and nano scale. I develop device models, analytical models, or numerical models to describe and explain these multiscale observations. The models also provide ways to predict device behaviour based on material properties.
