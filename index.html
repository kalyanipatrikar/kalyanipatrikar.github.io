Research

Graph Neural Network Predicting Molecular Properties

Representing molecular structures in the form of graph to learn correlations with ground state and excited state properties.

Microsopic Origins of Contact Resistance in OFETs

Ab initio simulations of charge transfer at organic-inorganic interface show how energy of interfacial states and orbitals and the extent of electronic coupling between them determine resistance, verified against measured devices.
Twitter

Mechanism of Self Assembled Monolayers

Experimental studies, first principles computations, and device models explain how self assembled monolayers modulate electron transfer at organic-inorganic interface.
Website Poster Twitter

Molecular Design for Rapid Charge Transport

Demonstration of the role of rigid side chain groups in enhancing mobility by modulating reorganization energy, and role of intermolecular interactions rather than bulk disorder in determining charge transport.
