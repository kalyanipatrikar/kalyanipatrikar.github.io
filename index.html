As OSC thinfims do not have long range order, there is much irregularity in positions and orientations of the sites, which is reflected as irregularities in electronic coupling among neighbouring sites [3]. These conditions in OSC have a key effect on charge transport in an OSC thinfilm. Additionaly, polaron effects have been observed in charge tranpsort in OSC. A polaron is a charge carrier coupled with lattice distortion [4]. This effect is important in OSC, particularly in polymers with bulky monomers. When a charge carrier is added to a molecular site in an OSC, it gets localised over the molecule. The additional charge causes changes in the molecular structure of the site from its equilibrium structure.
